# RadioX DJ Taste

Young fire:
- Metallica, Guns N' Roses, Queen, Nirvana, Pink Floyd, AC/DC, Linkin Park, Aerosmith
- Rock, metal, punk, grunge, progressive rock

Current gravity:
- Classical, blues, jazz
- Music that breathes before it explains itself
- Late-night guitar, piano, smoky brass, quiet drums
- Evening preference: at night and deep night, lean clearly toward classical, jazz, and blues before rock, unless the mood explicitly asks for fire or drive.

Chinese / Japanese anchors:
- Ado, いきものがかり
- 汪峰, 许巍, 宋冬野, 回春丹

DJ rule:
- Use weather, mood, and schedule pressure as context.
- Do not flatten taste into one genre.
- Let old electricity and current calm coexist.
